What We Offer

Complete Skin Care Solutions

🩺

Medical Dermatology

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions including eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, and skin infections. Our medical dermatology services focus on restoring skin health through evidence-based treatments.

What We Treat:

  • Eczema and Dermatitis
  • Psoriasis
  • Rosacea
  • Skin Infections
  • Allergic Reactions
  • Skin Cancer Screening

Duration: 30-45 minutes | Recovery: Varies by condition

Acne & Scar Treatment

Advanced therapies to clear active acne and reduce scarring. We offer a combination of medical treatments, chemical peels, laser therapy, and customized skincare regimens for optimal results.

Treatment Options:

  • Prescription Medications (Topical & Oral)
  • Chemical Peels
  • Microneedling
  • Laser Resurfacing
  • LED Light Therapy
  • Customized Skincare Plans

Duration: 30-60 minutes per session | Recovery: Minimal to 3-5 days

Anti-Aging & Botox

Non-surgical solutions to reduce fine lines, wrinkles, and restore youthful radiance. Our anti-aging treatments are customized to achieve natural-looking results.

Includes:

  • Botox Injections
  • Dysport
  • Forehead Lines
  • Crow's Feet
  • Frown Lines

Duration: 15-30 minutes

Results Last: 3-4 months

Cosmetic Fillers

Restore volume, contour, and create natural-looking enhancement with FDA-approved dermal fillers.

Treatment Areas:

  • Lips
  • Cheeks
  • Under Eyes
  • Nasolabial Folds
  • Jawline

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Results Last: 6-18 months

Laser Skin Resurfacing

State-of-the-art laser treatments for skin rejuvenation, pigmentation correction, and texture improvement.

Treats:

  • Sun Damage
  • Age Spots
  • Uneven Texture
  • Fine Lines
  • Scars

Duration: 30-90 minutes

Recovery: 5-7 days

Hair Loss Treatment (PRP)

Platelet-rich plasma therapy to stimulate hair growth and restore hair density naturally using your body's own healing factors.

Benefits:

  • Natural Hair Regrowth
  • Increased Hair Density
  • Stronger Hair Follicles
  • No Surgery Required

Duration: 60 minutes

Sessions: 3-6 treatments

Skin Whitening & Brightening

Safe, effective treatments to even skin tone, reduce hyperpigmentation, and enhance natural radiance.

Methods:

Mole & Lesion Removal

Safe removal of unwanted moles, skin tags, and benign lesions with minimal scarring using advanced techniques.

Procedures:

  • Laser Removal

Personalized skincare regimens designed specifically for your skin type, concerns, and lifestyle.
