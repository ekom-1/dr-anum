Common Questions

Before You Visit

No referral is needed. You can book an appointment directly with us for both medical and cosmetic dermatology services.
Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), list of current medications, and any relevant medical records. Arrive 10 minutes early to complete paperwork.
We accept most major insurance plans for medical dermatology services. Cosmetic procedures are typically not covered by insurance. Please contact us to verify your specific coverage.
Preparation varies by procedure. Generally, avoid blood thinners, alcohol, and sun exposure before treatment. We'll provide detailed pre-treatment instructions during your consultation.
Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you.
Yes, we offer complimentary parking in the Medical Plaza parking structure. Validation is available at our front desk.
